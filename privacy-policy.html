This Privacy Policy explains how Stag Creations (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile games and applications (“Apps” or “Services”).

By using our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile games and applications published by Stag Creations on Google Play and other app stores.

Our Apps are generally free-to-play games that may include advertising, analytics, attribution, app performance monitoring, diagnostics, and in-app purchases.

2. Information We Collect and Share

Our Apps may collect and share certain data automatically through the app itself and through third-party software development kits (“SDKs”) used for advertising, analytics, attribution, app performance, diagnostics, fraud prevention, security, compliance, and in-app purchase processing.

The types of data that may be collected and shared include:

2.1 Approximate Location

We and our third-party service providers may collect and share approximate location data, such as country, region, or city-level location inferred from device or network information.

This data may be used for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance

We do not collect precise GPS location unless clearly stated separately in a specific App.

2.2 Device or Other Identifiers

We and our third-party service providers may collect and share device or other identifiers, including advertising identifiers, app instance identifiers, device identifiers, and SDK-generated identifiers.

This data may be used for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Ad performance measurement
  • Attribution and campaign measurement
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance

2.3 App Activity

We and our third-party service providers may collect and share information about app activity, including app interactions and other actions performed in the App.

Examples may include:

  • App launches
  • Sessions
  • Gameplay events
  • Level or mission progress
  • Ad impressions and ad interactions
  • In-app purchase-related events
  • Other app usage events

This data may be used for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Attribution and campaign measurement
  • Improving gameplay, balance, and performance

2.4 App Information and Performance

We and our third-party service providers may collect and share app information and performance data, including crash logs and diagnostics.

This data may be used for:

  • App functionality
  • Analytics
  • Diagnosing technical issues
  • Improving app stability and performance
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance

2.5 Financial Information

If an App includes in-app purchases, we and our third-party service providers may collect and share purchase history and purchase-related information.

This data may be used for:

  • App functionality
  • Analytics
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Attribution and campaign measurement
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance
  • Processing, validating, and analyzing in-app purchases

We do not collect or store full payment card numbers. Payments are processed by the applicable app store or payment provider, such as Google Play.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

Unless clearly stated in a specific App, we do not collect the following types of data:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User IDs for account login
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • In-app search history
  • Photos or videos
  • Audio recordings
  • Files or documents
  • Calendar data
  • Health or fitness data

Our Apps do not currently allow users to create accounts, and users cannot log in with accounts created outside the App.

4. How We Use Information

We and our service providers may use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve our Apps
  • To provide app functionality
  • To display ads
  • To measure ad performance
  • To perform analytics
  • To measure installs, events, revenue, retention, and campaign performance
  • To process and analyze in-app purchases
  • To detect and fix technical issues
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, security issues, and policy violations
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and platform requirements

5. Third-Party Services

Our Apps may use third-party services and SDKs. These third parties may collect, process, and share data according to their own privacy policies.

Third-party services used in our Apps may include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play Services
  • AppLovin MAX
  • Tenjin
  • Advertising and mediation partners integrated through AppLovin MAX, Google AdMob, or other mediation platforms

These services may collect and process data such as approximate location, device identifiers, app interactions, other app activity, purchase history, crash logs, diagnostics, and app performance data.

For more information, please review the privacy policies and disclosures of the relevant third-party services:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google Play Payments
  • AppLovin Privacy Policy
  • Tenjin Privacy Policy

6. Advertising

Our Apps may show advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks and mediation partners.

Advertising partners may use device identifiers, approximate location, app activity, ad interactions, and other information to:

  • Serve ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Limit ad frequency
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Attribute installs and in-app events
  • Improve advertising campaigns

Depending on your region and device settings, you may be able to limit ad personalization through your device settings or consent choices shown in the App.

7. Analytics and Attribution

We may use analytics and attribution services to understand how users install, open, and use our Apps.

These services help us measure:

  • App installs
  • Sessions
  • Retention
  • Gameplay progress
  • Ad performance
  • In-app purchase performance
  • Campaign performance
  • Diagnostic and performance information

This helps us improve the Apps and evaluate advertising performance.

8. In-App Purchases

Some Apps may offer optional in-app purchases.

Purchase transactions are processed by the applicable app store, such as Google Play. We do not collect or store full payment card information.

We may receive and process purchase-related information, such as purchase history, product ID, transaction status, revenue amount, currency, and related analytics or attribution data, for app functionality, analytics, advertising measurement, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are intended for general audiences unless a specific App is clearly marked otherwise.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a way that is not permitted by applicable law. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent where required, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information.

Parents or guardians who believe that a child has provided personal information to us may contact us using the contact details below.

10. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

Data transmitted by our Apps and third-party SDKs is generally transferred using secure connections where supported by the relevant app, platform, and service providers.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including app operation, analytics, advertising, attribution, fraud prevention, security, compliance, and legal obligations.

Third-party service providers may retain data according to their own privacy policies and retention practices.

12. Data Deletion

Our Apps do not currently provide user accounts.

Because our Apps do not provide account creation or account login, we do not provide a direct in-app account deletion feature.

If you have questions about data associated with third-party services, you may review the privacy settings and policies of your device, app store account, advertising ID settings, and the third-party services listed in this Privacy Policy.

13. Your Choices

Depending on your device, platform, and region, you may have choices related to data collection and advertising, including:

  • Resetting or deleting your device advertising ID
  • Limiting ad personalization in your device settings
  • Managing consent choices shown inside the App, where applicable
  • Managing permissions through your device settings
  • Reviewing privacy settings in Google Play or your app store account

Some data collection may be required for app functionality, analytics, advertising, fraud prevention, security, compliance, or SDK operation.

14. International Data Transfers

Information collected through our Apps and third-party services may be processed and stored in countries other than your country of residence.

By using our Apps, you understand that information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers operate, subject to applicable laws and safeguards.

15. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the “Effective date” at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your continued use of our Apps after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
